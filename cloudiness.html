Latitude vs. Cloudiness



As expected, the weather becomes significantly wamer as one approaches the equatoer (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be wamer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This maybe due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
